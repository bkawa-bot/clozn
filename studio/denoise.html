watch a diffusion model think

A diffusion language model doesn't write left-to-right. It starts with a whole board of masked slots and fills them in parallel, refining over a handful of passes — each token landing with a confidence. The answer crystallises out of the noise. This is a real Dream-7B denoise, captured pass by pass.
